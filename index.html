Featured highlights

Tools that make decision-making fun

RapidChoice offers four main tools, each designed to solve a specific decision-making need. Explore every feature below, together with real in-app screenshots.

01 ‚Äî Wheels

Decision wheels: categories and weighted options

Whenever you cannot decide what to watch, where to eat or what to do at the weekend, RapidChoice wheels step in. Create a dedicated wheel for each category and give every option its own weight (voting power).

  • Create multiple categories and wheels
  • Add / edit / delete options and assign custom weights
  • Physics-based, fully animated wheel spinning
  • Confetti celebration when a winner is selected

02 ‚Äî Dice

3D dice rolling: tap, shake or press

The dice tool in RapidChoice uses realistic 3D animations and physics simulations. Roll one or two dice at the same time and clearly see the result on each face.

  • Roll with one or two dice
  • Tap, press the CTA button or shake your phone
  • Realistic 3D cube animations and lighting
  • Special confetti when you hit double six

03 ‚Äî Coin

Coin flip: the fastest tiebreaker

For small decisions, a classic coin flip always works. RapidChoice brings this experience to your phone with smooth 3D rotation and haptic feedback.

  • Flip by tapping the coin or pressing the button
  • Animated 3D rotation with random results
  • Clear result text and VoiceOver-friendly labels

04 ‚Äî Settings

Customizable settings and multiple languages

Tune RapidChoice to your preferences. Change the app language, theme mode and haptics in just a few taps.

  • Language options: English, Turkish, Spanish, Simplified Chinese
  • Separate toggles for haptics and (future) sound effects
  • "Rate RapidChoice" link and a lightweight About section
  • All settings stay on your device and persist between launches

05 ‚Äî Themes

Light, dark and auto themes

RapidChoice offers light, dark and system-based automatic theme options. The dark interface is perfect for late-night sessions, while the light theme keeps everything bright and clear when you prefer it.

  • Three theme modes: Light, Dark, Auto (system)
  • Colors tuned for comfortable contrast and readability
  • Your choice is saved in settings and remembered every time
